Hi! I'm Taavi Piigert

I am an aspiring Graphic Designer who strives for creative solutions in places and situations where they've been previously thought to be impossible. My primary focus has always been to stay practical with my solutions, while still straying away from the norm and staying unique.

My strongest skillsets lie within photo- and video editing, however I have also had thorough experience in audio production, 3D modelling and web design.
